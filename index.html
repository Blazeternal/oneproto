Monitors

Selecting the perfect monitor (or) desktop is very essential for perfect gaming experience.


Monitors with the smallest dot pitch are better. Look for a bright monitor with a lot of screen contrast.
Blacks should be opaque and the whites should be bright. Make sure the monitor is capable of the maximum resolution and color depth you ever expect to use with your computer,
including any graphics card or VRAM upgrades you may make.
